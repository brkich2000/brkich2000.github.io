More than 8.7 million adult

Australians do not hold a Will

The top 3 reasons for not having a Will;

  • - they do not feel they have sufficient assets to pass on
  • - Estate Planning costs too much money
  • - a Will is for old people

Since over 60% of all Australians do not hold a Will they risk a stranger deciding who should receive their assets and could result in families being torn apart or contesting the outcomes which means more cost and pain for everyone involved. Reel releases the video Will to the selected executors upon proof of death to ensure probate can be a quick and easy process to users estates being passed on in accordance with their wishes.

Reel's Mission

Our aim is to simplify the process, distance, timeframe and mind into a seamless interaction with our users contacts now and, in the future, and allow for clarity in communication in all areas of life and death.

Security

Reel uses a secure video digital Will with the use of Ethereum Blockchain to provide a new alternative to how our Wills are executed and providing further clarity as to why we have bequeathed our assets in the way we have.
